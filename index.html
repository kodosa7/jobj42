What

Yes, I'm turning 52 ... in base10. However, if you are like me and count in base13, it is 40. So sorry, no clean 42 this year, but I'm looking forward to 54 where it is base13 to get to 42. End of discussion.

Compo Rules

Deadline for compo's is Saturday 17h (20h for speedcompo), submit via mail or sneakernet. Provide a nick or artistname, name for your entry and short description in your entry (For the kidscompo; please include an age as well).

If you require Specific hardware (see list below) let us know BEFORE saturday afternoon, or bring your own.

The competitions wil be

  • Demo
  • Graphics
  • Music
  • Wild
  • Speed
  • Kids

Like last time, we have Demo Compos! Loosely based on the compo rules of Revision. But we want to be very inclusive to non Sceners so we accept Poems, Analog drawings, performances as well.

The compo PC is an i7 4900k, 32G ram, Nvidia RTX 3080, running Win10.

Examples: PC demo/intro, OldSkool Demo/intro, Fantasy platforms, Grafix, Music, Wild (A poem, perfomance art, short talk abnout a project you work on)

If you have a Linux entry, let me know in advance.

Oldskool platforms will be shown on an original hardware where possible. If not, I try to use an FPGA replacement. The rest will be shown using an emulator.

Remote entries possible, mail to me. Multiple entries allowed (within reason, and I decide what is reasonable).

For now we have infrastructure for the folowing competitions:

  • Oldskool Demo: BBC Micro/Master 128 (only tape, entries must be stored in WAV file), MSX1 (only cartridges at the moment), MSX2 (cartridge or floppy), real C64C with DigiMod and LumaFix and 1541U-II, C64-I with old SID, Atari 800XL with 572kB RAM (with SD card reader), Atari 800XE with 320kB RAM and stereo mod (8 channels), Atari 1040STF (with Gotek floppy), Atari Mega ST2 (with Gotek Floppy), Amiga 500 1.3 very early model with CF harddrive and 8MB FastRAM, Amiga 600 with Gotek or Floppy, Amiga 1200 with PiStorm, Amiga 1200 with original Blizzard 68060@50/64MB RAM, ZX Spectrum 48K, Timex Computer 2048 (basically ZXS 48K with better composite video and extra gfx mode), ZX Spectrum 128K (toastrack) with stereo sound mod, ZX Spectrum +2 grey with stereo sound mod, Sinclair QL with SD card solution, Sinclair Z88 portable computer (may be a challenge to upload a software to it), Atari 2600 with composite video and SD card solution (can run demos and homebrew), Atari Jaguar with composite video and BJL ROM loader (via serial PC port), Nintendo Wii with soft mod, OSSC (including composit/s-video to RGB expansion, RetroTINK 5X).
  • Streaming Music
  • Graphics Oldskool/Modern Graphics
  • ASCII/ANSI/PETSCII
  • PC Demo/Intro/4K
  • Fantasy Consoles Tic-80 Pico-8
