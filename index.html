Car
There are two parkings. PRIMARY, just close to the Brixel building and SECUNDAIRY, next to the Basisschool Spalbeek, accessible from Spalbeekstraat. This parking is about 1 minute walk to the party place. The entrance door is from the PRIMARY parking side.
Train
At the Hasselt train station, exit it and go left. Hop on one of two buses, Line 56 or Line 34. Use routeplanner with 'Hasselt Station P1' to 'Brixel Hackerspace Hasselt' for timetables. The bus ride takes about 26 minutes. Exit at 'Spalbeekstraat (56)' or 'Spalbeek Centrum (34)' and follow this map. The entrance door is from the PRIMARY parking side.

Compo Rules

Again, we have Demo Compos! Loosely based on the compo rules of Revision.

The compo PC is an i7 4900k, 32G ram, Nvidia RTX 3080, running Win10.

If you have a Linux entry, let me know in advance.

Oldskool platforms will be shown on an original hardware where possible. If not, I try to use an FPGA replacement. The rest will be shown using an emulator.

Remote entries possible, mail to me. Multiple entries allowed (within reason, and I decide what is reasonable).

For now we have infrastructure for the folowing competitions:

  • Oldskool Demo (C64 unless you bring your own hardware)
  • Streaming Music
  • Graphics
  • ASCII/ANSI
  • PC Demo/PC 64K
  • PC 4K/Tiny Intro
